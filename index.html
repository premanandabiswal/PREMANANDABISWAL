🐅🐯 Satkosia Tiger Reserve

Situated in central Odisha, Satkosia spans across four districts: Angul, Cuttack, Boudh, and Nayagarh. Established in 1976, covering 964 sq. km, and later declared a Tiger Reserve in 2007 under Project Tiger. The name Satkosia comes from “Sat Kosh” meaning seven kos (≈14 km) long gorge that the Mahanadi River cuts through the Eastern Ghats, creating breathtaking landscapes. The reserve is home to diverse flora and fauna, including the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger, Indian Elephant, and various species of deer, birds, and reptiles.

🏢 Modern Dhenkanal

Vedanta Group is establishing a large-scale aluminium smelter project in Dhenkanal, Odisha. This project, with an investment of Rs 1.28 lakh crore, includes a 3 million tonnes per annum (MTPA) aluminium smelter and a 4,900 MW captive power plant according to Odisha Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation. The project is expected to create 30,000 jobs and significantly boost the local economy through downstream industries and infrastructure development
